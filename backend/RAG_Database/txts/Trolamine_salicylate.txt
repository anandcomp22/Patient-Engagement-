Name: Trolamine salicylate

introduction: Adults and children 12 years of age : apply generously to the affected area, massage into painful area until thoroughly absorbed into the skin, repeat as necessary, but not more than 3 to 4 times daily. Children under 12 years of age : ask a doctor.

dosage_text: Temporarily relieves minor pain associated with arthritis, simple backache, muscle strains, sprains, and cramps.

how_to_use: For external use only . Allergy Alert: if prone to allergic reaction from aspirin or salicylates, consult a doctor before use. When using this product use only as directed, avoid taking a bath or shower within 1 hour before or after applying, do not bandage or use with a heating pad, avoid contact with eyes or mucous membranes, do not apply to wounds or damaged skin. Stop use and ask a doctor if condition worsens or symptoms persist for more than 7 days, if symptoms clear up and occur again within a few days, or if irritation develops.
