how_to_use: 16 HOW SUPPLIED/ STORAGE AND HANDLING Ecoza topical foam, 1% is white to off-white foam supplied in 10g (NDC 81811-100-10) and 70g (NDC 81811-100-70) aluminum pressurized canister. Store at controlled room temperature 20°C to 25°C (68°F to 77°F) with excursions permitted between 15°C and 30°C (59°F and 86°F). Do not refrigerate or freeze. Ecoza topical foam is flammable. Avoid heat, flame, and smoking during and immediately following application. Contents under pressure. Do not puncture and/or incinerate the containers. Do not expose containers to heat and/or store at temperatures above 120°F (49°C) even when empty. Do not store in direct sunlight.
