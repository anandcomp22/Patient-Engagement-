Name: Arsenic trioxide

introduction: Directions: FOR ORAL USE ONLY.

dosage_text: Dissolve pellets under the tongue 3-4 times daily. Ages 12 and older: 10 pellets. Ages 2-11: 5 pellets. Under age 2: Consult a doctor.

how_to_use: Warnings: Claims based on traditional homeopathic practice, not accepted medical evidence. Not FDA evaluated. Contains sugar. Diabetics and persons intolerant of sucrose (sugar): Consult a doctor before use. Do not use if allergic to any ingredient. Consult a doctor before use for serious conditions or if conditions worsen or persist. If pregnant or nursing, consult a doctor before use. Do not use if safety seal is broken or missing.
