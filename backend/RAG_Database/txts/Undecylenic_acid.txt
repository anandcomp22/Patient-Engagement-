Name: Undecylenic acid

introduction: Uses For the treatment of athlete´s foot.

dosage_text: Directions Wash the affected area and dry thoroughly. Apply a thin layer of the product over the affected area twice daily (morning and night) or as directed by a doctor. Supervise children in the use of this product. For athlete's foot: Pay special attention to spaces between the toes; wear well fitting, ventilated shoes, and change shoes and socks at least once daily. Use daily for 4 weeks. If condition persists, consults a physician. This product is not effective on the scalp or nails.

how_to_use: Warnings For external use only Do not use on children under 2 years of age, unless directed by a doctor. When using this product Avoid contact with the eyes. Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation occurs or if there is no improvement within 4 weeks. Keep out of the reach of children. If swallowed, get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away.
