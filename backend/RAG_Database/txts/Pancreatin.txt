Name: Pancreatin

introduction: Uses Helps promote and maintain healthy digestive functions and normal blood sugar levels. Directions To be taken 20 minutes away from food. Place drops directly under the tongue or into ¼ oz of water. Adults and adolescents (12 years and older): Take 10 drops two to three times daily or as recommended by your healthcare practitioner. Children (under 12 years): Take under the direction of your healthcare practitioner.
