Name: Xylometazoline

introduction: Adults and children 7 years and older: Spray into the nose 3 times a day

dosage_text: for nasal use

how_to_use: ■ When using this product Stop use and ask a doctor if nervousness, diziness, or sleepness occur. Pain, nasal congestion or cough gets worse or lasts more than 7 days. fever gets worse or lasts more than 3 days New symtoms occur. keep out of reach of children. In case of overdose, get medical help or contact a poison contral center right away. If pregnant or breast-feeding, ask a health professional before use.
